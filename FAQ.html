FAQ

1. DO we have trial period?

Answer: Yes, we actually do. you can try any plan for 30 days. then you’ll have to pay for every next month.

2. DO i have to link my card during registration?

Answer: Yes, and it’s necessary for registration. Don’t worry, you can always cancel your subscription 24/7. Also we’ll check your card by withdrawing 1 USD from it. It’ll return during 5 minutes, but if you don’t have any money in your balance, you won’t be able to register account.

3. Can i change my plan?

Answer: Yes, you can do it in any time and your present subscription won’t expire. When you change subscription plan, your current subscription will be active till the end, and only then your plan will be changed.

4. Is it allowed to use this hardware for mining?

Answer: No, it is forbidden. Every account that will use our hardware for mining will be banned permanently.
